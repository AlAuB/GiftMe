Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy for the "GiftMe!" app published by a group of students at Boston University. If you have any questions or concerns, you can contact us via our Support Form. All information on the responsible legal entity can also be found below under "Note on the responsible body".

General information

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you use this App. Personal data are all data with which you can be personally identified. Detailed information on the subject of data protection can be found in our data protection declaration listed below this text.

Data collection

Who is responsible for data collection in this Application

The data processing on this App is carried out by the App operator. You can find their contact details in the section "Note on the responsible body" in this data protection declaration.

How and why do we collect your data?

On the one hand, your data is collected when you communicate it to us. This can be, for. E.g. data that you enter in a contact form.

Other data are pulled automatically or with your consent when you use the App by our database systems. This part of data can include but not limited to: personal contact details, purchased items, etc. It all depends on what you may enter into the system. We collect those data for no public uses but only on a personal basis, which is for you and your friends to share information regarding your wishes and gifts. Data is collected through your local database SQLite and then uploaded to our Firestore Database.

What do we use your data for?

Some of the data is collected in order to ensure that the App is error-free. Other data can be used to analyze the user behavior and improve our app. We don't use your personal data outside the scope of this Application.

What rights do you have with regard to your data?

You have the right to receive information about the origin, recipient and purpose of your stored personal data free of charge at any time. You also have the right to request the correction or deletion of this data. If you have given your consent to data processing, you can revoke this consent at any time for the future. You also have the right to request that the processing of your personal data be restricted under certain circumstances.

You can contact us at any time if you have any further questions about data protection.
