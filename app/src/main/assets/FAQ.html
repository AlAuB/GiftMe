FAQ

- Q1: I can not add/see the wishlist.

  • In order to secure customersâ€™ information, customers need to sign in to add/see their wish lists and items.

- Q2: How can I sign in?

  • Go to the Setting page and press the sign in button. By using your Google account, you can sign in to our app.

- Q3: Can I use the app without the internet?

  • Currently, our app does not support the offline mode. Please use our app when you have the internet.

- Q4: How can I share my list with my friends?

  • Press the share sign at the top of the screen next to the wishlist name, then you can choose different ways to send the link to your friends.

- Q5: How can I receive a list from my friend?

  • Press the link that your friend sent; it will automatically add the list in the app. If you have a question about how to send the link, please check Q4.

- Q6: Can I see my wishlists on different devices?

  • Google Account protection mechanism prevents the same account from signing in on different devices, but it will show up under the same account if you only have one device signed in.

- Q7: Are there in-app purchases for this app?

  • Currently, we do not offer any in-app purchases.

- Q8: Can I see who collects the item in my wish list?

  • Claiming is an anonymous feature. In order to keep this as a surprise, you will not be able to see which items are claimed. Other friends cannot see who specifically is claiming your item.
